One-Pan Smores Skillet Cookie
Ingredients:
1 cup butter, melted
1 cup brown sugar
0.5 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla extract
2.5 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
0.5 tsp salt
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup mini marshmallows
5 graham crackers, broken into chunks

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350 F (175 C).
Mix butter and sugars until smooth, then add eggs and vanilla.
Stir in dry ingredients, then fold in chocolate chips, marshmallows, and graham crackers.
Press dough into a cast-iron skillet.
Bake 20 25 minutes until golden and gooey in the middle.
Serve warm with ice cream.
